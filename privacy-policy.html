4. AI Features & Third-Party Services

The Celestial Command Center includes AI-powered features: the Load Negotiation Coach, Rate Con Analyzer, AI Dispatch Assistant, and Maintenance AI Advisor. These features are powered by Anthropic's Claude AI.

  • When you use an AI feature, the load details or other data you enter are sent to Anthropic's API to generate a response
  • Your API key is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers
  • Anthropic may retain conversation data per their own privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy
  • Do not enter sensitive personal information such as Social Security numbers or full banking details into AI prompt fields

Other third-party services:

  • Open-Meteo — Free weather API. Provides weather data based on coordinates you search. No personal data is transmitted.
  • OpenRouteService — Commercial vehicle routing. Sends origin and destination coordinates to generate HGV-safe routes. No personal identifiers are sent.
  • Google Fonts — Loads display fonts. Google may collect standard web request data per their privacy policy.

5. GPS & Location Data

The Celestial Guide GPS navigation feature requests access to your device's location. Here is exactly how we handle that data:

  • Your location is accessed only when you actively use the GPS navigation feature and only with your explicit browser permission
  • Your GPS coordinates are used in real time to center the map and track your position along the route
  • Your location data is never stored on our servers and is not recorded or logged anywhere
  • Your live position is never sent to any third party — only your origin and destination are sent to OpenRouteService to calculate a truck-safe route
  • You can revoke location access at any time in your browser or device settings
Your control: The GPS feature only activates when you press "Start Navigation." It stops the moment you exit. We never access your location in the background.

9. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption on all pages of celestialdispatch.com
  • Session-based authentication that expires when you close your browser tab
  • No server-side storage of your Command Center data — it stays on your device
  • API keys stored only in your local browser, never transmitted to our servers
  • Access codes kept private and never displayed in plaintext after entry
Please note: No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we use commercially reasonable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Keep your access codes and API keys private and never share them with others.
