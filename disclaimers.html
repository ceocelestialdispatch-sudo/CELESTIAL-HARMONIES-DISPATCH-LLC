Celestial Harmonies Dispatch LLC makes no guarantee, expressed or implied, that:

  • Calculated rates will reflect actual live market rates at the time of any specific load booking.

  • Fuel surcharges, detention pay, accessorial fees, or deductions will match any estimated figures produced by the calculator.
